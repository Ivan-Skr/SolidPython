cylinder(r | r1, r2 | d | d1, d2; h; segments)

Creates a cylinder or cone centered about the z axis. When center is true, it is also centered vertically along the z axis. r - radius, r1 - bottom radius, r2 - top radius, d - diameter, d1 - bottom diameter, d2 - top diameter, h - height.

cylinder(r = x, h = x, segments = x)
cylinder(r1 = x, r2 = x, h = x, segments = x)
cylinder(d = x, h = x, segments = x)
cylinder(d1 = x, d2 = x, h = x, segments = x)
